Ten Affordable Restaurants in The Big Apple You MUST Visit

By Mahin Mahmud | Last Updated July 13th, 2024

New York City is renowned for its diverse and vibrant culinary scene, offering a plethora of dining experiences that can satisfy any palate. However, the city is also known for its high prices, making it seem challenging to find delicious yet affordable meals. Contrary to popular belief, NYC is home to numerous hidden gems where you can enjoy exceptional food without breaking the bank. This guide will introduce you to 10 such restaurants, proving that great taste and affordability can indeed go hand in hand in the Big Apple.

1. Top Thai

Top Thai is a beloved gem in New York City, offering an authentic and budget-friendly Thai dining experience. Known for its flavorful dishes and warm, inviting atmosphere, Top Thai has quickly become a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

  • Top Thai Greenwich: 235 Sullivan St, New York, NY 10012
  • Top Thai 55 Carmine: 55 Carmine St, New York, NY 10014

2. KPOT Korean BBQ and Hot Pot

If you're looking for a Korean barbecue experience in the city without having to drop your life savings then KPOT is the place for you.

  • Downtown Brooklyn: 430 Albee Square W, Brooklyn, NY 11201
  • Coney Island: 1217 Surf Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11224
  • Baychester: 200 Baychester Ave 207A 2nd Floor, Bronx, NY 10475
  • Howard Beach: 161-49 Cross Bay Blvd, Howard Beach, NY 11414

3. New HK Tea and Sushi

This lesser-known restaurant is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings. They have a large menu with a variety of options to choose from, many of which are under $20. They offer Hong Kong-style dishes as well as other Asian delights.

  • Bensonhurst: 2033 86th St, Brooklyn, NY 11214

4. Om Wok

Om Wok in Jackson Heights is a hidden gem that offers a delightful fusion of Asian cuisines, focusing on Thai, Chinese, and Indian flavors. This cozy and welcoming restaurant is known for its extensive menu, affordable prices, and high-quality dishes that are sure to satisfy a variety of tastes.

  • Jackson Heights: 78-14 Roosevelt Ave, Queens, NY 11372

5. 8-Bit Bites

8 Bit Bites is a nostalgic haven for gamers and food lovers alike, offering a unique dining experience that combines delicious comfort food with a retro gaming theme. Located in New York City, this quirky and fun eatery is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a meal in a lively and playful atmosphere.

  • Chelsea: 197 7th Ave, New York, NY 10012
  • East Village: 77 2nd Ave, New York, NY 10003
  • Downtown Brooklyn: 113 Court St, Brooklyn, NY 11201
  • Bushwick: 881 Lexington Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11221
  • Bed-Stuy: 967 Bedford Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11205

6. Shawarma Mia

If you're looking for affordable and delicious Middle Eastern food, Shawarma Mia is the spot for you. Similar to Chipotle, Shawarma Mia offers a make-your-own meal experience. You can choose from a variety of meats such as beef, lamb, and chicken, along with a wide range of toppings.

  • Prospect Heights: 67 6th Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11217

7. Mahmoud's Corner

I know this isn't a sit-down restaurant like the others on the list, but I just had to add it. Mahmoud's Corner isn't your typical halal cart around the city; it is MUCH better. Most people have probably heard of The Halal Guys or Adel's Famous Halal Food, but many haven't heard of the superior cart: Mahmoud's Halal.

  • Astoria: 34th Ave, Queens, NY 11103

8. Wok Wok Southeast Asian Kitchen

Wok Wok South East Asian Kitchen is a vibrant and inviting restaurant in New York City that specializes in a diverse range of Southeast Asian cuisines. Known for its authentic flavors, generous portions, and affordable prices, Wok Wok offers a delightful dining experience that transports you to the bustling streets of Southeast Asia.

  • Chinatown: 11 Mott St, New York, NY 10013

This casual eatery has gained a loyal following thanks to its commitment to quality and affordability, making it a perfect choice for a satisfying meal without breaking the bank.

  • Hell's Kitchen: 649 9th Ave, New York, NY 10036
  • Lower East Side: 124 Ludlow St, New York, NY 10002
  • Downtown Brooklyn: 158 Lawrence St, Brooklyn, NY 11201
  • Flatbush: 942 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11226
  • Bayside: 213-05 Northern Blvd, Queens, NY 11361

10. Birria-Landia

Birria-Landia is a beloved food truck in New York City that has earned a stellar reputation for its authentic and flavorful birria tacos. This culinary gem brings the rich, savory taste of traditional Mexican birria to the streets of NYC, offering a delicious and affordable dining experience that has captured the hearts of food enthusiasts across the city.

  • Jackson Heights: 77-05 Roosevelt Ave, Jackson Heights, NY 11372
  • Williamsburg: 491 Metropolitan Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11211
  • Upper West Side: 234 Amsterdam Ave, New York, NY 10023
  • Bowery: E Houston St, New York, NY 10002
  • Fordham: 2501 Grand Concourse, Bronx, NY 10468
